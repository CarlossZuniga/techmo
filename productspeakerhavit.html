Information

The Havit Portable Bluetooth Speaker is designed to offer high-quality sound in a compact and durable form. Equipped with advanced audio drivers, this speaker delivers clear highs, rich mids, and powerful bass, ensuring a well-rounded listening experience. Its Bluetooth 5.0 technology provides a stable and fast connection to your devices, with a range of up to 100 feet. The speaker features a built-in microphone for hands-free calls and a rechargeable battery that offers up to 12 hours of continuous playtime on a single charge. Its rugged design is IPX7 rated, making it water-resistant and suitable for outdoor use. The Havit speaker also includes convenient features like a TF card slot and an AUX input, providing multiple ways to enjoy your music. Whether you're at home, at a party, or on an adventure, the Havit Portable Bluetooth Speaker is a reliable companion for all your audio needs.
